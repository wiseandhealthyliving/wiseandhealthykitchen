Why We Only Recommend Safe Materials

Some appliances use synthetic coatings and plastics that may degrade under high heat. We focus on material transparency and durability.

BPA & Phthalates: Hormone disruptors found in plastics
PFAS (Teflon): "Forever chemicals" linked to health issues
Microplastics: Released from heated plastic components

We test and recommend only stainless steel, glass, and ceramic appliances that keep your family safe.
