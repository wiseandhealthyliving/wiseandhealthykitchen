Some Points on Air Fryer Safety

Pioritize material transparency, construction quality, and long-term durability.

Interior Materials

Stainless steel interiors and ceramic coatings should always br carefully considered. Products using traditional non-stick PTFE coatings are reviewed with material disclosures in our reviews.

Chemical & Coating Transparency

Examine manufacturer documentation for PFAS, BPA, and coating certifications. Cross-check claims against publicly available safety standards.

Build Quality & Heat Performance

Always consider heat distribution, structural integrity, and long-term wear information. Safety includes durability, not just marketing labels.
